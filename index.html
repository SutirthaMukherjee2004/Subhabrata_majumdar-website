Hello!

I am Subhabrata Majumdar, a Professor in the Department of Theoretical Physics at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai.

My research group works on cosmology, galaxy clusters, the cosmic microwave background, and large-scale structure, combining theory, statistics, and multi-wavelength survey data to test our model of the universe.

Research Interests

  • Cosmology and large-scale structure
  • Galaxy clusters and mass calibration
  • Cosmic microwave background and secondary anisotropies
  • Astro-particle physics and dark matter phenomenology
  • Survey science, baryons, feedback and statistical inference
